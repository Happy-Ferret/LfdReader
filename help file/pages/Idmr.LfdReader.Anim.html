Remarks

The Animation class is heavily used in the concourse and menus. Sliding doors, talking heads, even many of the buttons are composed of animation frames. How fast the animations run, if they're on repeat, what frame they start at, etc are all controlled by the FILM resource. Really, the ANIM is just a collection of DELTs, as such Anim uses the Delt class to actually process the image data. At this time the editing capability is rather limited, but future projects will require additional functionality.
