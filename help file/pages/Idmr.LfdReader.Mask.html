Remarks

This is used for cockpit transparencies and is a simple black and white image. Typically a little shorter than 640x480 (leaves room for messages along the bottom) this mates up with a cockpit view so you can see space through it. If you really want to, you can just blank the whole thing and it'd be like flying snowspeeders before it was fixed in the Special Edition.
