Remarks

Global position is referenced to screen 0,0 whic is the upper left corner.
Think of a large picture frame on a wall, and then place a wallet photo on it. That wallet photo has its own X/Y coordinates relative to the wall, and those are the coordinates stored. The exceptions are thrown if the edge of the wallet pass the edge of the picture frame.
