Remarks

This is something I've come to expect from the old Lucasarts/Totally Games partnership, but here we have two resource types that are exactly the same. Maybe they're handeled by different processes within TIE's source code, but they are structurally the same, hence a single class to take care of BLAS and VOIC. Typically, BLAS is sound effects, and VOIC is used for voices. The resource itself is just a container for a VOC audio file, and for our purposes composes of uncompressed 8-bit audio which is very easy to convert to WAVE. Currently the BLAS-WAVE conversion is handeled by the TIE Sound Editor, but at a later date I may wrap that functionality into LfdReader.
